## Design & Tone Guidelines

- **Tone:** Professional but approachable. Technical credibility without jargon overload.
- **Language:** English primary. No French on the site (except if a blog post is in French).
- **Design priority:** Content-first, minimal, fast. Imperfect design is fine — focus is on shipping content.
- **Dark mode:** Nice to have, not blocking.
- **No:** Animations, carousels, preloaders, modals. Keep it simple and fast.
